Hey Princess !

Since the day you entered my life, it was as if someone had opened a curtain on a boring, monotonous existence. You didn't only make my days brighter you orchestrated them. You transformed silence into music, empty space into page, and transitory moments into strokes of something timeless. Being with you is like being within a painting that's still coming to life full of action, significance, and enchantment I never want to end. You are not merely an artist. You are art. Your soul is painted in mad, unutterable colors colors too exotic for words, too vivid for description. Your being takes up a space like sunlight streaming through colored glass: broken, blinding, and unmissable. Your laugh is acrylic paint on board rich, textured, and alive. Your voice is charcoal on paper soft but intense, leaving deep lines even at its most subdued. You find beauty where others do not. You see light in the dark, order in disorder, tales in silence. To watch you work the way you become lost in a drawing, the way your hands dance on brushes, the way your eyes observe the world as if it were a living museum it is not just moving. It is religious. Today, the world honors the day you were born but I honor something far greater: how you live. How you love. How you make the mundane the unforgettable. You are chiaroscuro light and dark in exquisite harmony. You are negative space softly strong, defining everything you touch. You are perspective noticing layers others can't see. You are texture never two-dimensional, never predictable, always genuine. You are the deepest work I've ever come across not only to be admired from afar, but to be held near, learned from, loved. So on your birthday, my prayer for you is this: May your life be as lovely as all the loveliness you bring to the world in your painting, in your smile, in your love. May your canvas roll out across forever. May you never lose the nerve to paint in your colors, to draw in your own voice, to leave your mark boldly. Because the world is richer, and fuller, and more true with you inside of it. . You smile like sunlight passing through colored glass broken, radiant, and indelible. Today is not only the day you were born it's the anniversary of the day the universe signed its most gorgeous self portrait: you. I gaze at you and see chiaroscuro your depth of kindness in the shadows, your radiance of passion in the light. I see you building love like brushstrokes on canvas slow, deliberate, intentional. Even your silences are like sketches of something divine in progress. So here's my birthday wish for you: May your days be as rich as your color, your dreams as free as your imagination, and your heart always filled with people who perceive the work that you are and love each stroke, every smudge, every beautiful imperfection. Happy Birthday, my muse. You are my favorite art work not only to view, but to admire endlessly. Happy Birthday, my muse. You are my favorite creation not of my hands, but of fate’s finest design. And loving you? That’s the greatest masterpiece of all.
